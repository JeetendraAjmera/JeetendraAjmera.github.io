About Me

Hi, my name is Jeetendra Ajmera, a 22-year-old storyteller based out of the alluring Pink City of Rajasthan who is on a quest to spread the art of storytelling around.

Working on stories means that I love to narrate, read, teach and write stories.Storytelling is an art that has been practised since time immemorial. It is an ever pervasive art, whether we look up to the arenas of mythological epics or the current world of digitalisation, it has been there ingraining our minds and souls with opinions, beliefs, tales and narratives. The Power of Storytelling is built around the idea that stories can change our world as they are the primary force of human connection and hence our most powerful tool for learning to live a better life.
